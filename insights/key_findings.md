## 🔍 Key Findings

- **Revenue grew 12× from Jan 2017 to Aug 2018**, with a distinct Black Friday spike in Nov 2017 (+87% MoM)
- **Top 3 categories** (`cama_mesa_banho`, `beleza_saude`, `esporte_lazer`) account for ~28% of total revenue
- **93% of delivered orders arrived within SLA**, but 3.2% were more than 7 days late — concentrated in the North/Northeast states
- **Top-quartile sellers** average 4.2× the revenue of bottom-quartile sellers, with only marginal differences in review scores
- **RFM segmentation** reveals ~8% of customers are "Champions" generating 31% of total revenue
